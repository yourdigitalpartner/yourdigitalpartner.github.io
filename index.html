What is AEO (Answer Engine Optimisation)?

AEO is the practice of optimising your website and content so AI assistants like ChatGPT, Gemini and Perplexity cite your brand when answering buyer questions. It combines structured data (schema), answer-formatted content, FAQs and technical accessibility for AI crawlers.

How is AEO different from SEO?

SEO earns you rankings in traditional search results; AEO earns you citations inside AI-generated answers. They overlap heavily — strong SEO foundations help AEO — but AEO adds AI-crawler access, entity clarity and answer-first content structure. The best strategies do both, which is exactly how I work.
