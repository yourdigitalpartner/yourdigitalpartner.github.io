SEO, AEO and AI visibility: questions answered.

What is AEO in simple terms?

AEO, or Answer Engine Optimisation, is the process of improving how easily AI search tools can find, understand and use information from your website. It works alongside SEO and focuses on technical accessibility, clear entities and services, useful answers, credible evidence and content that addresses the questions buyers ask.

How is AEO different from SEO?

SEO improves visibility in traditional search results. AEO focuses more specifically on how your business is understood and surfaced within AI-generated answers, recommendations and citations. They share the same foundations, so AEO should normally be part of a strong SEO strategy rather than treated as a separate replacement.

How can I get my business mentioned or cited by ChatGPT?

There is no setting that guarantees a ChatGPT citation. The strongest foundations include allowing relevant search crawlers to access your public pages, clearly explaining your services and expertise, publishing useful original content, supporting claims with evidence and earning credible mentions elsewhere on the web.

Can you guarantee AI citations?

No consultant can guarantee that a particular AI platform will mention or cite a business. Results vary by platform, prompt, location, user context and changes to the underlying systems. My role is to identify and improve the factors most likely to strengthen your visibility, then measure changes across a consistent set of relevant prompts.

What does an AEO audit include?

The audit covers technical SEO, crawl and index accessibility, AI-search crawler access, structured data, entity clarity, service-page content, internal linking, expertise signals and visibility across selected buyer prompts. You receive a prioritised scorecard with the three actions I would address first.

How long does AEO take to work?

There is no fixed timeframe. Technical and on-page changes can be discovered after pages are recrawled, while improving visibility for competitive commercial topics often requires sustained content, evidence and authority building. Progress should be measured from a clear baseline rather than promised against a universal deadline.

Which AI platforms do you assess?

The audit can assess representative prompts across ChatGPT, Gemini, Perplexity and relevant Google AI search experiences, alongside traditional Google rankings and organic performance. The exact platforms and prompts should be selected according to where your prospective customers are most likely to research suppliers.

How do you measure AI visibility?

I track whether your brand is mentioned, recommended or cited across a consistent set of commercially relevant prompts, how visibility changes over time and how you compare with selected competitors. This is assessed alongside organic rankings, traffic, conversions and referral visits from AI platforms.
