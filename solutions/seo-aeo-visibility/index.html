What is AEO in simple terms?

AEO (Answer Engine Optimisation) is making your website easy for AI assistants — ChatGPT, Gemini, Claude, Perplexity, Copilot — to read, trust and cite. When a buyer asks an AI for a recommendation, AEO is what determines whether your brand is part of the answer.

Do I still need SEO if I'm doing AEO?

Yes — they're two sides of the same strategy. AI assistants lean heavily on content that already ranks well and demonstrates authority, so strong SEO foundations directly improve your chances of being cited. I build one visibility strategy that wins in both.

How long does it take to show up in AI answers?

Technical fixes (schema, AI-crawler access, answer-first restructuring) can start influencing citations within weeks, because AI tools retrieve live web content. Building authority for competitive prompts takes longer — typically a 3–6 month content programme, prioritised by commercial value.

How do you measure AEO success?

By tracking the prompts that matter to your pipeline: whether AI assistants mention or cite your brand, how you compare to competitors on the same prompts, and referral traffic from AI tools — alongside classic SEO metrics like rankings and organic conversions.
