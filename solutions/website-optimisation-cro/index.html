Edit 01 · Show, don't tell

Swap the stock image for a video demo — gated by just an email.

Session recordings showed visitors scrolling straight past the hero. A short product demo behind a single email field turns passive scrolling into your highest-intent conversion point — no seven-field form required.

Result: +32% demo requests
Edit 02 · Speak to the person

One headline for everyone becomes a persona-matched message.

Your analytics already know who's visiting. Persona-matched headlines, content and CTAs mean a marketing leader and a finance director each see the value that matters to them — personalisation that lifts engagement across every page.

Result: 2.3× content engagement
Edit 03 · Let them self-serve

A wall of text becomes an interactive solution finder.

Heatmaps show which sections get used and which get ignored. Where attention dies, interactive modules — solution finders, product quizzes, calculators — pull visitors in and route them to the right page in seconds.

Result: +40% self-serve journeys
Edit 04 · Remove the toll booth

Seven form fields become one — and leads start flowing.

Every field you ask for costs you leads. Funnel analysis finds exactly where drop-off happens; progressive profiling collects the rest later. Shorter forms, smarter follow-up, measurably more pipeline.

Result: −16% drop-off, +18% MQLs
